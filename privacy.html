🔒 Privacy Policy

Nebula — Space Shooter · Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and shared when you play Nebula — Space Shooter (the "Game").

1. Information We Collect

The Game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. However, the following third-party services integrated into the Game may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements (banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads). AdMob may use the Android Advertising ID, device identifiers, and approximate location to serve personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Play Services — required for ad serving and in-app billing. May collect device and account information as described in Google's privacy policy.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements (banner, interstitial, and rewarded video)
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Provide future optional in-app purchases

3. Advertising ID

The Game uses the Android Advertising ID via AdMob to serve personalized ads. You can reset or opt out of advertising personalization at any time via:

  • Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Third-party ad networks (Google AdMob) may receive anonymized data through standard ad-serving processes.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is rated Everyone 10+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service, contact us and we will assist in removal.
